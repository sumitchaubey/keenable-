Your Internship Journey

Here's what you can expect during your time with us:

  • Stage 1: Foundational Immersion (1 Month)

    • Focus: Building a strong foundation for success through non-technical but essential skills.
    • Key Learnings:Communication and Collaboration: Mastering effective communication, teamwork, and conflict resolution.
    • Project Management: Understanding project lifecycles, planning, and task management methodologies.
    • Agile Methodologies: Introduction to agile principles and practices relevant to DevOps and development teams.
    • Professional Development: Setting personal goals, building time management skills, and enhancing professional communication.
    • Industry & Company Insights: Gaining a comprehensive understanding of the tech industry landscape and company's mission, values, and culture.
    • Activities: Interactive activities, team-building exercises, guest speaker sessions, company culture immersion activities.
    • Benefits: Strong communication, collaboration, and project management skills are invaluable for any developer or DevOps professional. This stage prepares interns for effective teamwork and navigating project complexities.


  • Stage 2: Project-Based Exploration (4 Months)

    • Focus: Embarking on independent projects, applying learned skills, and demonstrating technical expertise.
    • Project Choice: Interns select projects aligned with their interests and career goals, with mentor guidance. Projects can focus on DevOps automation, development of new features, improving existing systems, or exploring emerging technologies.
    • Mentorship: Dedicated mentors provide support, guidance, and feedback throughout the project development process.
    • Technical Documentation: Interns create comprehensive technical documentation for their chosen projects, showcasing their understanding and communication skills.
    • Deliverables: Completed projects, technical documentation, and presentations demonstrating learning and achievements.
    • Benefits: Interns gain hands-on experience, build their portfolios, and explore specific technologies in-depth. Project documentation enhances communication and technical writing skills.
